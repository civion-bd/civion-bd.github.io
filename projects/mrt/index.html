MRT Line-5 Northern Route

Geotechnical FEA

Scope

Project Overview

Geotechnical risk quantification for TBM-induced ground movement on MRT Line-5 Northern Route alignment. Static, dynamic, and liquefaction analysis of the urban soil profile.

Project Details

ClientDMTCL (Dhaka Mass Transit Company Ltd.)
LocationDhaka, Bangladesh
CategoryGeotechnical
Method

Technical Approach

  • check_circlePLAXIS 3D FEA calibrated from field borehole data
  • check_circleConstitutive models: HS-Small (clayey soils), UBC3D-PLM (loose sand / liquefaction), Norsand (dense sand / dilatancy)
  • check_circleDynamic TBM loading cycles: vibration propagation profiles, static settlement contours, cumulative residual settlement
  • check_circleLiquefaction screening on susceptible strata within influence zone
  • check_circleDefined safe TBM operation corridors replacing conservative blanket exclusion zones
