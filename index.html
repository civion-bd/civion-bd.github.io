Who We Are

An engineering practice, not a drafting shop.

Civion BD covers the full project lifecycle — geotechnical investigation data, structural analysis and design, BIM coordination, and visualization — under one roof, so nothing gets lost in translation between disciplines.

We were founded by two BUET civil engineering classmates with complementary specializations, built to be code-versatile across Bangladeshi (BNBC 2020) and international (ASCE 7-22, ACI 318, Eurocode, ISO 19650) practice.
